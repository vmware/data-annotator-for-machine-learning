What is {{env.config.serviceTitle}}?

{{env.config.serviceTitle}} helps data science teams facilitate the creation and management of annotations for your machine learning project.

Tutorials and Kaggle challenges provide clean labeled data and score on model performance. This is often not the case in the real world where “real data” requires much more than modeling. In addition to data pre-processing, successful ML projects often require annotating a lot of data.

{{env.config.serviceTitle}} streamlines the entire annotation process so you can centrally create, manage and administer annotation projects. No more sharing confusing large spreadsheets and bugging annotators over Slack/Email.

Image, text, tabular and logs are supported.
